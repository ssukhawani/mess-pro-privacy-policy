3. Permissions and Access

To provide certain features, Mess Pro requires access to specific device permissions:

  • Camera:
    • Purpose: To scan QR codes for meal plan verification and coupon redemption.
    • Usage: The camera is accessed only when you use features that require QR code scanning. We do not store or share any images captured by the camera.
  • Location:
    • Purpose: To enable location mapping for in-app activities, such as finding exact place for the mess.
    • Usage: The location is accessed only when user allows that input. We do not store or share anything without your explicit consent.

Note: You can manage these permissions through your device settings at any time.
